SOURCES

Our main focus during the whole forming and developing process of ShareSafe was to have Good Accessibility. The content is without a doubt important, but if the information isnâ€™t mediated in a easy and universal manner, then the purpose of good content dissolves. From the beginning we used sites like W3 and W3-School to create a webpage that is easy to access and user friendly to all.

Good contrasts are important for users with impaired vision, we have used Leaveou for our colour choices. In addition we have used Checkers to measure our site with the guidelines for Web Accessibility. Our Goal was to achieve the highest possible accessibility benchmark wich is 90% or higher. This qualifies as a Web page with good accessibility.

For our image handling we have decided to use so called free stock photos. These are images which are not protected by the copyright and are available for everyone under the CC0 policy. Here we mainly used Pexels.com
